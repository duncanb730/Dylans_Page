Learning Experience

I was born November 1st, 2014. My first act in the world was to bite the doctor. My dad thought I was a little blue when I first showed up but I got better pretty quickly. I was strong from the beginning, and I surprised the photographer by doing pushups on my third day outside. My parents had photos taken of me pretty soon. Most people seem to like them.

First Halloween

This was his first Halloween. He was born on November 1, and his mother went into labor for him on Halloween night. We took this photo at a local farm with a pumpkin patch. Dylan was close to walking then and he stayed in the patch for a little while just learning to stand up. I wonder what he thought about seeing pumpkins almost bigger than him and definitely heavier than him.
